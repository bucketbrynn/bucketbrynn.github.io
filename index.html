Projects

My biggest coding project to date has been the CareCoord app, my senior capstone project. CareCoord is an app designed to make shared-caretaking easier. We (the Caretechers) were inspired to make this app when we saw our parents struggling to take care of their elderly relatives and coordinate that care with their siblings.

As part of the Caretechers team, I am mainly backend-focused, and I have been in charge of creating and maintaining a MySQL database, writing an Express.js backend/API, and creating API documentation with Swagger. There is one other backend developer, and two front-end developers.

The CareCoord app is currently in the Beta phase, with all core functionality completed. We are entering development for the Final phase, in which we will be polishing and adding features that give it a little extra "oomph". You can view CareCoord here.
